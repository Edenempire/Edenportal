THE VISIONARY

In the beginning, before the foundations of the Conglomerate were laid, there arose a young male adult named Thato Morake, a son of the soil, born under the African sun with fire in his bones and vision in his spirit.
And the young Thato looked upon the world and saw that it was bound by smallness, by the chains of limited thinking, by minds that could not stretch beyond the horizon of their own sight.
But Thato was not like the others. For within him burned a flame that could not be quenched — the flame of empire, the fire of dominion, the unrelenting desire to build something greater than himself.
He spoke to himself in the quiet hours, saying, "Why should I inherit leftovers when I can sit at the head of the table? Why should I follow when I was born to lead?"
And the Lord, who sees the hearts of men, looked upon Thato and was pleased, for his ambition was not for vanity but for purpose — to create, to build, to establish a legacy that would outlive his bones.
Thus the seed of Eden was planted in secret, in the soil of a young man's soul, waiting for the appointed time to sprout forth.
🔥 "From one spark, a nation shall be kindled."

THE YEAR OF DIVINE APPOINTMENT — 2023

Now it came to pass in the year of our Lord 2023, in the city of Durban, at the sinister universal church, that the hand of Providence moved to weave together the threads of destiny.
For Thato had long been a faithful among the congregation, a familiar face in the assembly, known to many but truly known by few.
But there came also one Philani Kemele, a young male adult of soldier, code and logic, whose mind ran in obliteration, circuits and algorithms.
And 2023 was Philani's first year at that place. He came as a stranger, as one unknown, carrying within him gifts he himself did not yet fully comprehend.
At first, Philani looked upon Thato with skeptical eyes, saying in his mind, "yeah... another charmer boy that thinks the world revolves around him."
Little did he know that the very skepticism that guarded him would soon become the foundation of an unshakeable alliance.

THE CANCELLED SOCCER TOURNAMENT

And it came to pass that the sinister church proclaimed a soccer tournament, a gathering of the young boys to compete in the beautiful game.
The boys assembled, and they began to practice together, their feet upon the grass, their hearts stirred by the prospect of victory.
Now Philani had no love for soccer in those days. His world was the world of ones and zeros, of compilers and interpreters, of logic gates and data streams.
But Thato and one Mpumelelo spoke, saying, "Join us. What matters is not your skill but your presence."
And Philani heeded their voice, though reluctance weighed upon his steps. He went to the practice, not knowing that this simple act would alter the course of his life forever.
Between passes, they spoke of God. Between tactical formations, they spoke of manhood. Between goals, they spoke of purpose.
And with every word that Thato spoke, Philani felt the walls of his skepticism crumble: "This one is different. This one thinks as I think."
But the bishop Hermano Souza cancelled the tournament, saying: "Why feed the flesh rather than the spirit?" And everyone was mad. Yet from that cancellation, a greater fire arose.
Thato envisioned Parables and Platters. Philani pushed for a December launch. They called Masiya — who could cook — for they only knew how to fry eggs. They sold at Umhlanga with success, until Masiya lost the re-stocking money.
Thus they paused. And Philani re-focused on military duty — an act of God to preserve the defender of the nation. The tournament died, but Eden breathed its first breath.
⚡ "From every cancellation, a greater genesis is born."

THE VISION OF THE BLACK HORSE

In the first month of 2024, God came to Philani in a vision: He saw the Son of Man, Jesus Christ, black as Africa, riding on a black horse on the seashore.
And Jesus said: "I have a special military operation for you. From February to September. Will you accept?" Philani answered without hesitation: "Lord, send me."
Then the Lord declared: "He who overcomes shall sit with Me in My throne." By September 2024, Philani passed the operation at 100%.
When he returned, they relaunched Parables and Platters in March 2025 with paperwork. On the first night they made over +1K, though transport costs (R400 daily to Leroy) cut deep.
Thato paid for a base in town. Philani became the cook — he had learned during his hidden season. They sold around Durban Central. Eden stretched its limbs.
🐉 "He who is faithful in the hidden shall govern in the open."

THE LIBRARY MEETING

Philani sent word to Thato: "Let us meet at the Durban Central Municipal Library." And they sat together in that place of books and silence.
Philani asked: "Where do you want to be in life?" Thato answered: "The top." Philani replied: "Me too."
"How do we get there?" — "Through money." — "And how do we get that money?" — "Business."
In that library, two young men bound themselves to a vision that would become Eden Conglomerate. One builds, one defends. One speaks, one calculates. And Eden rose.
